About Us





OurCedi is a platform for the history of Ghana. We are passionate about letting the world know about our culture and way of life. Here we talk more not just the cedi but the remarkable history of how our country Ghana has survived and became what we experience today.






OUR VISION






To lead the way in promoting and preserving the rich cultural heritage of Ghana through the story of the Cedi. Ghana is a nation steeped in history, and the Cedi is a symbol of that heritage.






OUR MISSION

Our mission is to educate and inspire individuals about the significance of the Cedi and its role in Ghana's history. We aim to create a platform for dialogue and exchange of ideas about our cultural heritage. To provide resources and information that will help individuals understand and appreciate the Cedi and its importance to Ghanaian identity.
